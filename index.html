Welcome to my site! I'm a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences. Prior to joining Verily, I received a BA in Applied Mathematics at UC Berkeley and a PhD in Biostatistics at Harvard University under the direction of Professor Tianxi Cai. I was also a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine with Professor Lu Tian.

I am interested in the application of data science to healthcare problems. Most of my work focuses on the development of statistical methods for routinely collected digital data such as electronic health records and mobile health data.
