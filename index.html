Welcome! I'm a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences. Prior to joining Verily, I received my PhD in Biostatistics at Harvard University under the direction of Professor Tianxi Cai. I was also a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine with Professor Lu Tian.

I am particularly interested in the application of data science to healthcare problems. Most of my time is spent developing statistical methods to leverage routinely collected digital data such as electronic health records and mobile health data.

When I'm not doing statistics, you'll likely find me running, cooking, or walking my dog, Teddy.
