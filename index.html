I am a biostatistician turned health data scientist.

My work focuses on machine learning and statistical inference methods for electronic health records and mobile health data.

Currently, I'm an assistant professor in the Department of Statistical Sciences at the University of Toronto. Before joining U of T, I was a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences.

I received a BA in Applied Mathematics at UC Berkeley and a PhD in Biostatistics under the direction of Tianxi Cai at Harvard University. I did my postdoctoral work with Lu Tian in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine.
