I'm a biostatistician working on health data science.

Most of my work focuses on the development of machine learning and statistical inference methods for routinely collected digital data such as electronic health records and mobile health data.

Currently, I'm a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences. I will be joining the Department of Statistical Sciences at the University of Toronto in July 2020.

I received a BA in Applied Mathematics at UC Berkeley and a PhD in Biostatistics at Harvard University under the direction of Tianxi Cai. I was also a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine with Lu Tian.
