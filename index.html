I am a biostatistician working on machine learning and statistical inference for healthcare applications.

I'm currently a data scientist at Alphabet's Verily Life Sciences. Prior to joining Verily, I received a BA in Applied Mathematics at UC Berkeley and a PhD in Biostatistics at Harvard University under the direction of Tianxi Cai. I was also a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Biomedical Data Science at the Stanford School of Medicine with Lu Tian.

Most of my work focuses on the development of statistical methods routinely collected digital data such as electronic health records and mobile health data.
